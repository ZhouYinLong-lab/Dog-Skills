## Theoretical Foundation

The methodology is based on the Stanford d.school course *"Designing Your Life"* and the book *《斯坦福大学人生设计课》* (Designing Your Life: How to Build a Well-Lived, Joyful Life) by:

- **Bill Burnett** — Former Apple designer (designed durable hinges for early Apple laptops), Executive Director of Stanford's Life Design Lab
- **Dave Evans** — Former Apple designer (co-developed Apple's first mouse), Co-founder of Stanford's Life Design Lab, Lecturer at Stanford d.school

The prompt also integrates:

- **Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi's Flow Theory** — 心流理论
- **Martin Seligman's Positive Psychology** — 积极心理学
